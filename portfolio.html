Social Tv Prototype

Social TV is a television which supports communication and social interaction in either the context of watching television or related to TV content (Social television, 2013). Social TV enables people to communicate with friends while watching TV shows. Moreover, a wide variety of information related to TV content is accessible synchronously and asynchronously. For instance, while watching a football match, viewers are able to see old scores of specific teams, background information about teams, and links to buy uniforms of certain teams. Furthermore, they can share these types of information easily through social networks such as Facebook, twitter, etc., via their mobile and tablet devices.We develop a prototype for Social Tv with Sarah Drine, Pavlo Bazilinskyy and Albert Liu.

Obstacle Avoidance Robot Simulation

This project aims to create a robot simulation which can reach given target point in space with obstacles.Two kinds of algorithm implemented;
Potential field algorithm with obstacle avoidance method: Obstacle force will be considered in order to move towards target. Obstacle force increases in inverse proportion to the distance to the nearest obstacle boundary.
RRT algorithm with obstacle avoidance method: Algorithm will check that whether an expansion of a new node is going to touch the obstacle or not. If it is touching the obstacle, iteration will repeat until it finds a free space on map.
